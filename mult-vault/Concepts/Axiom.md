# Axiom

An [[Axiom]] is an accepted assumption that does not have a proof inside the current system.

Example:

```text
Assume: every nonempty type has a chosen element.
```

This is a form of the axiom of choice.

Axioms are not automatically bad, but they change what a theorem depends on. In proof assistants, it is often useful to know whether a theorem was proved constructively or using extra axioms.
